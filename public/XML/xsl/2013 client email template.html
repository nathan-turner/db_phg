  ABOUT US

Pinnacle Locum Tenens is part of Pinnacle Health Group (PHG), a full-service physician recruitment firm in its 20th year of operation. We realize that filling your staffing needs is like a relay race, with the end result being a fully-staffed department. Pinnacle Health Group's locum tenens and permanent departments work as a team to help you complete each leg of the race. We can provide initial coverage to meet your immediate needs and at the same time work to find your facility the perfect permanent solution. As a result, PHG can help clients start, accelerate, and finish the race to meet their placement needs, often in record time.
